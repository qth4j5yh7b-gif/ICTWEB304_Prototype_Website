ESP32-C3 Zero Case Design Project

This website showcases the design process for creating a case for the ESP32-C3 Zero. This project’s main goal is to develop and test a 3D printed protective cover for the ESP32-C3 Zero mini-controller. The case must protect the product for regular classroom use such as protection from dust, small droppages and prevent handling of sensitive electronics with bare hands whilst also retaining the free use of all ports and features such as buttons. One of the main problems that we are trying to solve for, would be developing a design that is not too bulky, yet still retains enough strength to protect the case.

This website will document the design iterations, testing results, and the final solution. Please feel free to navigate through the different prototypes and the final design. There will be a feedback form available for you to share your thoughts and suggestions at the bottom of this page. Thank you for your time and interest in our project.
